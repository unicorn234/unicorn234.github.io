Workspace

Electronics

Electronics such as phones can be distracting because if you get a message from your friend while working, its likely you will pick up your phone to chat with your friend. This causes you to lose attention on your work and pay attention to interruptions. You are being interfered by technology. If you are using an electronic device for work, it is important that you block your distractions on it.

No Electronics

  1. You will concentrate on your work
  2. You finish your assignments on time
  3. You won't feel like getting off-task
  4. You can take a break after you are done with your work
